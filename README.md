## Project Overview

Under Armour is a global sportswear and perfomance apparel company founded in 1996. The company specializes in athletic clothing, footwear, and accessories desinged to improve perfomance. Its mission is to make athletes better through passion, design, and innovation. The target audience includes athletes, gym enthusiasts, and young adults interested in fitness and fashion. 

## Website Goals and Objectives

The main goal of the website is to create  a strong online presence and showcase Under Armour products in a user-friendly way. The website aims to:

1. Promote products and brand identity
2. Provide product information
3. Improve customer engagement

Key perfomance indicators (KPIs) include:

1.Number of website visitors
2.User interaction (clicks and navigation)
3.Time spent on website
